This blog support two type of users:

Regular Users can:

  • View list of all "Published" blogposts
  • View list of their own "Published" posts and "Drafts"
  • Create new blogpost "Drafts" (which then need to be approved and published by Admin)
  • Edit/Delete their own blogposts
  • Comment blogposts
  • Edit/Delete their own comments

You may register a new user via SignUp page here Signup

Admin User can:

  • Do everything that a Regular user can
  • Publish post "Drafts"
  • Delete other user's blogposts
  • Delete other user's comments
